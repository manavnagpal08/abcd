Job Title: Logistics Coordinator

About Us:
We are a leading company in supply chain and transportation looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to ensure efficient and timely movement of goods across our supply chain.

The Role:
As a Logistics Coordinator, you will be responsible for coordinating shipments and transportation logistics, managing inventory and warehouse operations, and communicating with carriers, suppliers, and customers. You will work closely with logistics and operations team to optimize delivery schedules and reduce shipping costs. This role requires a strong commitment to efficiency and precision.

Key Responsibilities:
- Coordinate domestic and international shipments, including scheduling, documentation, and tracking.
- Manage inventory levels, oversee warehouse operations, and ensure accurate record-keeping.
- Communicate effectively with carriers, suppliers, and internal teams regarding shipment status and issues.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Logistics, Supply Chain Management, Business Administration or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in Logistics Coordinator or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Logistics Coordination, Inventory Management, Freight Forwarding.
- Demonstrated experience with Microsoft Excel, ERP/WMS Systems, Communication Skills, Problem Solving.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Customs Regulations, Incoterms, Transportation Management Systems (TMS).
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- ensuring seamless product delivery and customer satisfaction.
