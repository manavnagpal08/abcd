Job Title: Research Chemist

About Us:
We are a leading company in chemical research and development looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to discover and develop novel chemical compounds and processes.

The Role:
As a Research Chemist, you will be responsible for designing and conducting chemical experiments, analyzing experimental data and interpreting results, and developing new chemical synthesis routes or formulations. You will work closely with R&D chemistry team to advance scientific understanding and develop innovative chemical solutions. This role requires a strong commitment to scientific rigor and innovation.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and execute complex organic or inorganic synthesis experiments in a laboratory setting.
- Utilize analytical techniques (e.g., NMR, LC-MS, GC-MS, FTIR) to characterize compounds.
- Analyze and interpret experimental data, troubleshoot issues, and propose next steps.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry, or Chemical Engineering or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in Research Chemist or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Organic Synthesis, Analytical Chemistry, Laboratory Techniques.
- Demonstrated experience with NMR, LC-MS, GC-MS, Data Analysis, Scientific Method.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Polymer Chemistry, Materials Science, Process Chemistry, Chromatography, Spectroscopy.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- contributing to scientific breakthroughs and new product development.
