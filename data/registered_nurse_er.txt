Job Title: Registered Nurse (ER)

About Us:
We are a leading company in emergency healthcare services looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to provide compassionate and critical care to patients in emergency situations.

The Role:
As a Registered Nurse (ER), you will be responsible for providing immediate and comprehensive patient care in a high-acuity environment, assessing patient conditions and prioritizing interventions, and collaborating with a multidisciplinary team to ensure optimal patient outcomes. You will work closely with Emergency Department nursing team to deliver high-quality, life-saving care under pressure. This role requires a strong commitment to patient safety and rapid response.

Key Responsibilities:
- Conduct rapid and accurate patient assessments, triage, and prioritize care based on acuity levels.
- Administer medications, perform treatments, and monitor patient responses in critical situations.
- Collaborate effectively with physicians, residents, and other healthcare professionals to develop and implement care plans.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Nursing or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in Registered Nurse or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Emergency Nursing, Patient Assessment, Triage, IV Insertion.
- Demonstrated experience with ACLS, BLS, PALS, Critical Thinking, Medication Administration.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with CEN certification, Electronic Medical Records (EMR), Trauma Nursing.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- making a direct and immediate impact on patient lives in critical moments.
