**Job Title: Senior Software Engineer (Backend)**

**Location:** Bengaluru, India

**About Us:**
We are a fast-growing tech company building innovative solutions for the fintech industry. We are looking for a highly motivated and experienced Senior Software Engineer to join our dynamic backend team.

**Responsibilities:**
* Design, develop, and maintain robust, scalable, and high-performance backend services and APIs.
* Lead the implementation of new features and improvements using Python and modern frameworks (e.g., Flask, Django).
* Work with cloud technologies, specifically AWS (Lambda, EC2, S3, RDS, DynamoDB), to build resilient and efficient systems.
* Collaborate closely with product managers, frontend developers, and QA engineers in an Agile/Scrum environment.
* Implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines to ensure continuous delivery and deployment.
* Write comprehensive unit, integration, and end-to-end tests.
* Participate in code reviews, mentor junior engineers, and contribute to architectural discussions.
* Troubleshoot and debug production issues, ensuring high availability and performance of applications.

**Required Skills & Qualifications:**
* Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
* 5+ years of professional experience in backend software development.
* Strong proficiency in **Python** and its ecosystem.
* Extensive experience with **AWS** cloud services (Lambda, EC2, S3, RDS, DynamoDB).
* Solid understanding of **RESTful API** design and microservices architecture.
* Experience with **SQL** and NoSQL databases (e.g., PostgreSQL, MongoDB).
* Familiarity with **Docker** and **Kubernetes**.
* Experience with **CI/CD** tools (e.g., Jenkins, GitHub Actions).
* Proficiency in **Git** for version control.
* Strong understanding of **Agile** development methodologies (Scrum, Kanban).
* Excellent problem-solving, analytical, and communication skills.

**Preferred Qualifications:**
* Experience with other programming languages like Java or Node.js.
* Knowledge of data streaming platforms (e.g., Kafka).
* Contributions to open-source projects.
