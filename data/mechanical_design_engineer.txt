Job Title: Mechanical Design Engineer

About Us:
We are a leading company in product development and engineering looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to design innovative and high-performance mechanical systems.

The Role:
As a Mechanical Design Engineer, you will be responsible for designing mechanical components and assemblies, performing engineering analysis and simulations, and creating detailed CAD models and drawings. You will work closely with mechanical engineering team to deliver robust and manufacturable mechanical designs. This role requires a strong commitment to precision and innovation.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and develop mechanical components and systems using CAD software (e.g., SolidWorks, AutoCAD).
- Perform engineering calculations, FEA, and simulations to validate designs.
- Create detailed engineering drawings, specifications, and BOMs for manufacturing.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering or a related engineering discipline or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in Mechanical Design Engineer or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in CAD Software (SolidWorks, AutoCAD), Mechanical Design, FEA (Finite Element Analysis).
- Demonstrated experience with Prototyping, DFM (Design for Manufacturability), GD&T (Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing).
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Matlab, Python for engineering analysis, CAM software, PDM/PLM systems.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- innovating and bringing tangible products to life.
